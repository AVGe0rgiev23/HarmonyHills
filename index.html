Frequently Asked Questions

Is the apartment pet-friendly?

+

Yes! We welcome pets with a small additional deposit. Please let us know about your furry friend when you apply.

What's included in the rent?

+

All utilities are included: electricity, water, heating, cooling, high-speed internet, parking, and building maintenance.

How long is the minimum lease?

+

We offer flexible lease terms with a minimum of 6 months. Longer leases may qualify for discounts.

Is a security deposit required?

+

Yes, we require a security deposit equal to one month's rent, which is fully refundable upon move-out.
